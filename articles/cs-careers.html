CS Careers: What's Next?

Computer science opens the door to an incredible variety of career paths. Here’s an overview of what’s possible and how to get started early.

Software Development

Software developers design, build, and maintain applications that people use every day. From apps on your phone to web platforms and video games, developers shape the digital world.

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

AI specialists build systems that can learn, predict, and make decisions. Fields like computer vision, natural language processing, and robotics are booming with opportunities.

Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity experts protect sensitive data from hackers. With the increasing importance of digital security, this field is both challenging and rewarding.

Data Science & Analytics

Data scientists transform raw data into actionable insights. They work in business, healthcare, sports, and almost any industry that relies on data to make decisions.

How to Get Started

Start learning coding early. Build small projects, participate in competitions, and write essays or blog posts about what you’re learning. Resources and practice are key to developing skills and landing opportunities in CS.
