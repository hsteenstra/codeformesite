Local Roots, Local Impact

Code For ME was built in Bath, Maine to give local students a way to learn coding without traveling far or paying expensive fees. We believe the best programs are created by people who know the community and show up for it.

That local energy is what makes Code For ME feel different — a welcoming place to learn, grow, and build confidence.

About

Meet The Founder

Hi! I'm Harper Steenstra, the founder of Code For ME- a local coding camp designed for beginner coders. I believe coding is essential to the future. Whether you're building a toaster or designing the next iPhone, code is behind it all.

I first discovered coding back in third grade, dragging colorful blocks across the screen. I instantly fell in love. But once that class ended, I realized how few programming opportunities were available for students like me.

After years of learning computer science through online and in-person courses, I'm now ready to help your child start their coding journey. Thank you for being here — and welcome to Code For ME!

We personalize experiences with custom-made visuals and posters that help concepts stick and make learning feel approachable.

A Letter To Teachers

This textbook is designed specifically for teachers who are new to coding. It provides a complete, ready-to-teach 5-day introduction to Python that you can bring directly into your classroom.

Our goal is to support you every step of the way. With step-by-step lessons, visuals, and structured activities, you’ll feel confident teaching even if you’ve never coded before.

This program is built so that you learn alongside your students. As they explore Python, you’ll be gaining the same understanding, helping create a collaborative and engaging learning experience.

CodeForME: Coding Made Simple For Every Classroom is a 5-day beginner program for ages 12+, created to make computer science approachable, engaging, and accessible in any classroom.
